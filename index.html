Masao Oi (大井 雅雄)

I am a program-specific assistant professor (Hakubi project) at Kyoto University.
I am interested in Number Theory, especially representation theory of p-adic groups.
I received my doctor's degree in 2019 from the university of Tokyo under the supervision of Yoichi Mieda. [CV]
e-mail: masaooi at math.kyoto-u.ac.jp
